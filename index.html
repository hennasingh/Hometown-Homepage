Visit Amritsar

Enjoy culture, delicacies and historical tales in this vibrant city of Punjab.

Top three activities to do at Amritsar

Take a historical visit to Jallianwala Bagh

Jallianwala Bagh is a historic garden and memorial of national importance.

Tune in to Holy Hymns at Golden Temple

The most significant shrine in Sikhism. An open house of worship for all people, from all walks of life and faiths.

Enjoy the famous Amritsari Street Food

Street Food in Amritsar is unmatched! You can take a foodie out of Amritsar, but you can't take Amritsar out of a foodie.
